Bias Tape Face Mask

Description

This is a standard pleated face mask with bias tape ties and a flexible nose piece.

Requirements

Materials

  • 2 pieces of Quilting Cotton (tightly woven cotton fabric) 9" x 7"
  • 2 pieces of Bias Tape 54''
  • 5-6'' Floral Wire (Optional, for flexible nose bridge)
  • Sewing Thread

Tools

  • Sewing Machine
  • Scissors
  • Iron
  • Needle and Pins
