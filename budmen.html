Budmen Face Shield

Description

3D Face Shield to be used as temporary replacement for critical situations as a Personal Protection Equipment for healthcare professionals during the COVID 19 pandemic.

Requirements

Materials

  • Foam Tape 3/4in wide x 1/2in thick - cut to 9.5in long
  • .005in thick poly sheet - cut to 12in x 9in rectangles Material: PET-G, Acetate, or other clear impermeable material.
  • Elastic Band - 1/2in wide - cut to 31in long
  • PLA Filament for 3D Printer
  • Super Glue
  • Rubbing Alcohol and other disinfectants
  • Polybags (for packaging)

Tools

  • 3D Printer
  • .25in hole punch
  • Razor blade or scissors
  • Vinyl Gloves
  • Face Mask

Assembly Instructions

Isaac Budmen and Stephanie Keefe of Budmen Industries make and sell custom 3D printers, have stopped sales in order to produce devices for health care workers facing the COVID-19 pandemic. They have made available free of charge, the 3D design templates and instructions for the manufacture of face shields.
