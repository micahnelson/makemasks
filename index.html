Our heroes need masks.

Fight the Covid-19 PPE Shortage

Healthcare providers are in urgent need of PPE (Masks, Gloves, Face Shields etc). If you have certified PPE donate it to a local hospital or medical facility.

Existing supply may not be enough to protect our health care workers and care givers. There will be supply in a few weeks, but there is a critical need now. You can make emergency PPE for your local health care providers right now, following the patterns listed below.
