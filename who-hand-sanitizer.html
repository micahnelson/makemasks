WHO Hand Sanitizer

Description

While hand sanitizer is not regarded as PPE, it is used in many health care facilities and nursing homes who often depend on retail suppliers. This formula is based off of the World Health Organization's guidance.

Requirements

Materials

  • Isopropyl alcohol (Instructions for 90 and 70%)
  • Hydrogen peroxide
  • Glycerin
  • Pure Water (If using 90% or higher alcohol)
  • Label
  • Spray Bottle

Tools

  • Eye Protection
  • Mixing Container

Assembly Instructions

Presented by Youtube user G Chang based on World Health Organization guidelines.
