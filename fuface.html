Fu Face Mask

Description

Popular and easy pattern from an open source project called FreeSewing run by Joost De Cock (joost.decock.org) a “sewcialist” and programmer.

Requirements

Materials

  • Basic sewing supplies
  • About 15 cm (6”) of a suitable fabric (tightly woven cotton or viscose/rayon)What fabrics are best for mask making?
  • About 15 cm (6”) of lining fabric
  • About 1.2 meter (4 feet) of ribbon cut in 4 equal parts

Tools

  • Scissors
  • Sewing Machine

Assembly Instructions

Freesewing is an open source platform for made-to-measure sewing patterns by Joost De Cock.
